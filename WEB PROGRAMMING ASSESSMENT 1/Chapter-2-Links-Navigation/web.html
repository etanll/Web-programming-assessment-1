The Pacific (2010)

The Pacific (2010) is a 10-part HBO war drama miniseries produced by Steven Spielberg, Tom Hanks, and Gary Goetzman, designed as a thematic companion piece to Band of Brothers. It provides a raw, unflinching, and gritty portrayal of the United States Marine Corps’ actions in the Pacific Theater during World War II.
