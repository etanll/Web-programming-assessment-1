EGG FRIED RICE RECIPE

This is a quick and tasty egg fried rice recipe made with rice, eggs, garlic, and soy sauce. The rice is stir-fried together, then garnished with green onions and sesame oil for extra flavor. This recipe is simple, fast, and tasty.

THE INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups of cooked cold jasmine rice
  • 3 large eggs
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 chopped green onions
  • 2 tbsp of Vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil

THE COOKING METHOD:

  1. Prepare the rice: Break up any clumps in a bowl
  2. Prepare the wok by adding heat and the vegetable oil
  3. Add 3 large eggs to the wok and scrable
  4. Add in 4 garlic cloves and saute
  5. Add in the rice and mix it in
  6. Add 2 tbsp of soy sauce to the rice and mix
  7. Sprinkle 1/2 tsp of salt and mix
  8. After cooking serve hot and add chopped green onions and sesame oil as garnishing
